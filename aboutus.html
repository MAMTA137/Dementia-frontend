About us

Our goal is to improve the quality of life for demetia people to achieve a better everyday life. A little more joy, more robust and less stress for there loved one. Demetia app is designed so that it simulates some of the issues and techniques a psychologist would use in his or her first meeting with an individual seeking help.

Why we made this?

we made this app to make easy communication between doctor, dementia patient and there caretaker. About what food patient consumed, photos of patients loved one, medicine, todo list and many more.
